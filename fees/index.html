Frequently asked questions

Find answers to the most frequently asked questions about Exness fees.

Is Exness free?

We work to keep costs as low as possible for our clients, removing many of the expected charges implemented by other brokers. Like all online trading platforms, Exness charges spreads and small commissions on selected instruments.

Are all payment transactions free?

At Exness, we don't apply any charges or commission to any deposits or withdrawals. Third-party payment providers may impose charges, but we pay those too, so you don't have to. There are a small handful of exceptions to this, including miner's fees on cryptocurrency payments, deposits with USDT, and withdrawals with Perfect Money and Skrill.

Do I have to pay swap fees?

While most brokers charge swaps or overnight fees, we decided to eliminate them for our most popular instruments. Stocks, crypto, indices, gold are all swap-free, along with some popular currencies.

Does Exness charge management fees?

No. We don't charge any management fees and it's completely free to open an account with us.
